Game Boy Promotions Through the Years

1989-1990: Launch Promotion

Nintendo launched the Game Boy with the tagline "The Greatest Handheld of All Time!" The device came in a standard gray color and was marketed as an affordable, durable gaming device.

1998: Game Boy Color Release

Nintendo introduced the Game Boy Color with full color support. Multiple color variants were available, including transparent, atomic purple, and neon colors. This promotion targeting nostalgic gamers and new audiences significantly boosted sales.

Bundled Games

Tetris was bundled with many Game Boy units, leading to massive adoption. Other popular bundled titles included The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening and Mario Land, making the device an incredible value proposition.

Carry Cases and Accessories

Nintendo promoted protective cases, screen protectors, and link cables for multiplayer gaming. These accessories were heavily marketed to enhance the Game Boy experience.
