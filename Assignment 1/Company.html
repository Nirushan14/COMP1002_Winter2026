About Nintendo

The Gameboy Division

Nintendo released the Game Boy in 1990, revolutionizing portable gaming. The Game Boy became the best-selling handheld gaming device of all time, with over 118 million units sold across all variants. Its success helped establish Nintendo as a leader in handheld gaming technology.

Innovation and Legacy

The Game Boy's success was built on durability, battery life, and a strong game library. Nintendo continued to innovate with improved models like the Game Boy Color, Game Boy Advance, and eventually the Nintendo DS, which maintained the Game Boy's legacy as a handheld gaming powerhouse.
